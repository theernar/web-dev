Recipe #1: Omelette

  • Time: 7–10 min
  • Serves: 1
  • Level: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs2
Milk (optional)2 tbsp
Saltpinch
Butter / oil1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs + salt (add milk if you want).
  2. Heat pan, add butter/oil.
  3. Pour eggs, cook 2–3 min, fold and serve.
